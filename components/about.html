About Legacy Electrical

Our Story

Founded with a commitment to quality and reliability, Legacy Electrical has established itself as a trusted electrical contractor serving Connecticut.

Based in New Britain, our company brings professional expertise and dependable service to every project we undertake. We take pride in our reputation for excellent workmanship, timely service, and customer satisfaction.

Meet Our Team

Led by owner Tayler Nadolny-Sipes, our team of skilled electricians combines years of industry experience with continuous training in the latest electrical systems and safety protocols.

As a fully licensed and insured electrical contractor (License: ELC.0207936-E1), we take pride in maintaining the highest standards of workmanship and professionalism in every job we complete.
